About the Project

Dhule Clinic is a specialized healthcare facility designed to provide accessible and quality medical services to the Dhule region. With Nayesha Healthcare's planning and design expertise, it offers comprehensive outpatient and diagnostic services.

Our Contribution

Nayesha Healthcare provided clinic planning, infrastructure design, operational setup, and quality management systems. We ensured the facility meets all regulatory requirements while maintaining cost-effectiveness.

Services Provided

Clinic Planning & Design • Infrastructure Development • Operational Management • Staff Training • Quality Systems • Patient Experience Enhancement

Key Achievements

Successfully established a modern clinic with outpatient facilities, diagnostic services, and specialized consultation rooms. Achieved high patient satisfaction and operational efficiency.
