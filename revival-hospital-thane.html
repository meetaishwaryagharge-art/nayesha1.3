About the Project

Revival Hospital, Thane is a modern multi-specialty healthcare facility designed to provide comprehensive medical services to the Thane community. Built with Nayesha Healthcare's expertise, it combines advanced technology with patient-centric care.

Our Contribution

Nayesha Healthcare provided end-to-end hospital planning, infrastructure design, operational setup, and quality management systems. We ensured compliance with all regulatory standards while optimizing operational efficiency.

Services Provided

Hospital Planning & Design • Infrastructure Development • Operational Management • Staff Training • Quality Systems • Revenue Optimization • Patient Experience Enhancement

Key Achievements

Successfully established a 80+ bed multi-specialty hospital with advanced diagnostic facilities, 24/7 emergency services, and specialized departments. Achieved operational excellence and high patient satisfaction ratings.
