About the Project

Shree Hospital, Karad is a modern healthcare facility designed and developed with Nayesha Healthcare's expertise. The hospital combines cutting-edge infrastructure with compassionate patient care, setting new standards in healthcare delivery in the region.

Our Contribution

Nayesha Healthcare provided comprehensive hospital planning, infrastructure design, operational setup, and NABH accreditation support. We ensured the facility meets international standards while maintaining cost-effectiveness and sustainability.

Services Provided

Hospital Planning & Design • Infrastructure Development • Operational Management • Staff Training • NABH Accreditation • Quality Systems Implementation • Revenue Cycle Optimization

Key Achievements

Successfully established a 100+ bed multi-specialty hospital with modern diagnostic facilities, emergency services, and specialized departments. Achieved NABH accreditation and established best-in-class operational protocols.
